    Virtual camera crop with 4K support

    Create virtual cameras from any camera input. Ideal for 4K inputs when streaming in 1080p or 720p. Easier camera moves, cuts and presets supported.

    Bonded Streaming

    Now out of beta, bonded streaming* brings you a more stable streaming experience by making it possible to spread your live stream over multiple internet connections, including Ethernet, Wifi, 3G/4G LTE modems, or any other connection you can configure in Windows. You can also set a backup internet connection that will be used only if your primary internet connection fails or becomes too slow, avoiding expensive data charges.
